Meroë

Meroë is a fictional sudanese restaurant that I came up with for a university project. I named it Meroë because Meroë was the capital of the Kingdom of Kush for several centuries and there are ancient pyramids and other items of historical significance at the location. I made use of the shapes of the pyramids in the logo, used colours drawn from the sandy enevironment, and used a font that has design elements inspired by Arabic writing.
