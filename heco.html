Heco Analytics

Heco Analytics is a health analytics company. I produced a logo, icon set and website for them. The logo visualised a network of interacting data. The icons visualised different data sets and were used on the site and in the software. The website made use of negative space and the icons to break up the text heavy content to give the site a clean and clinical appearance as the client is in the medical sector.
