BRIEF: THE FUTURE NOW

The brief for this project was to create a brand for a future organisation that represented robotics or relationships between robots and humans. BEEP. (Breaking Enslavement of Electronic Persons) is a Trade Federation for sentient robots. I have designed a welcome pack including a welcome document, a brochure for their affiliated Trade Union, EPU (Electronic Persons Union), a Manifesto Poster and a Membership Card.
