Living Data

Living data is an exhibition designed to educate people on the positives of Big Data and how it is changing society. Big data has changed the world. We can now track the spread of diseases, treat illnesses more effectively, improve performance of ourselves, products and services.

Our new exhibition explores how society is changing in response to the developments of big data technology. How is human health and performance improving with big data? How is personal transport and goods transport becoming more efficient and effective? How are businessses increasing returns and reducing wastage? These are all questions that are answered in this exhibition.
